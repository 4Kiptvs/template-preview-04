Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you start streaming in 8K.

Is STRONG 8K IPTV legal?

IPTV technology is completely legal. The legality depends on the specific content you access. We provide connection services to publicly available streams. Please verify content regulations in your specific region before subscribing.

What devices support 8K streaming?

8K streaming requires an 8K-capable TV and sufficient internet speed (at least 100Mbps recommended). For standard 4K/FHD, any Smart TV, Firestick, iPhone, iPad, Android device, Windows PC, Mac, or MAG box works perfectly.

Do you offer free trials?

Absolutely. We offer complimentary trial periods so you can experience the 8K quality difference before committing. Contact us via WhatsApp and we'll set you up with a full trial immediately.

How quickly will I receive my account?

Account delivery is instant — typically within minutes of payment confirmation. Our team operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to ensure you're streaming as fast as possible.

What internet speed do I need for 8K?

For 8K streaming we recommend a minimum of 80–100 Mbps. For 4K, 25 Mbps is sufficient. For HD/FHD, 10 Mbps works perfectly. Our adaptive streaming adjusts automatically to your connection speed.

Do you provide installation support?

Yes — our 24/7 WhatsApp team will guide you through installation step-by-step on any device. We offer both text tutorials and live video assistance. Average setup time is under 5 minutes.
